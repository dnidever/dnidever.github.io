I am an Associate Professor at Montana State University and I also work with the Astro Data Lab group at CSDC on large-scale photometric surveys. My research is focused on understanding the structure, formation and evolution of our galaxy and its largest satellite galaxies, the Magellanic Clouds, using several large surveys.
